Test Steps

  1. Launch the web app named "schemescheck"
  2. Click the link "geo" to launch map app
  3. Click the link "mailto" to launch email app
  4. Click the link "open market" to launch Market app
  5. Click the link "send sms" to launch message app
  6. Click the link "make call" to launch Tel app

Purpose:

Verifies that enable scheme 'app://', 'mailto:', 'market:', 'sms:', 'tel:', 'geo:'

Pre-condition:

  • Connect to network
  • Map Application is installed on device such as google map

Expected Result:

  1. The web app is launched successfully
  2. The map app could pop up after click the link
  3. The email app could pop up after click the link
  4. The Market app could pop up after click the link
  5. The Sms app could pop up after click the link
  6. The Tel app could pop up after click the link
