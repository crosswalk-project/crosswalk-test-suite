Launch sub app "IAP Google Play Demo", choose "Google Play" and buy some in-app products.


Pre-condition:

  1. GMS (Google Mobile Services)
    IAP is provided by GMS, which means the device must have Google Play and Google Wallet installed.
    For Nexus 5 device, which is shipped Android 4.4 KitKat, they have been integrated in the system.
  2. Google account
    Make sure Google Play has been installed in the Android system. Make sure you can access Google Play. A Google account with an available Google Wallet which should been granted as a tester of the test app org.xwalk.iapdemo from Google Play.
  3. Sign the iapdemo.apk use iapdemo.keystore before the test.
    This test requires the apk running as release mode, so you must sign it before test. Please use the iapdemo.keystore as the key to sign the apk.
  4. No GFW
    IAP is not provided in China mainland, we must find a way to go over GFW to test IAP functionalities. (Maybe in the future, Google Play returns China mainland, then we can test this case without the aid of VPN!)

Verify to query the product details and buy the in-app product successfully.
