Scan for media renderers

Test Purpose:

Check if can scan for media renderers successfully, and operate media renderers successfully


Test Steps:

  1. Click the 'Scan for media renderers' botton, to scan for media renderers
  2. Wait for scaning media renderers successfully
  3. Click the 'Open' botton, to open a media on the renderer
  4. Click the 'Prefetch' botton, to preload a media on the renderer
  5. Click the 'Cancel' botton, to cancel all requests has outstanding on the renderer
  6. Click the 'Play' botton, to play the renderer's current media
  7. Click the 'Pause' botton, to pause the media renderer
  8. Click the 'Stop' botton, to stop the media renderer
  9. Click the 'Next' botton, to play the next track of the renderer's playlist
  10. Click the 'Previous' botton, to play the previous track of the renderer's playlist
  11. Click the 'Mute' botton, to mute the media renderer
  12. Click the 'setSpeed' botton, to set the media renderer's play speed
  13. Click the 'setVolume' botton, to set the media renderer audio volume
  14. Click the 'gotoTrack' botton, to open the specified track number of the renderer's playlist

Expected Result:

Test passes if scan for media renderers successfully, and can operate media renderers successfully
