Purpose:

Validate the scheme 'app://', 'mailto:', 'market:', 'sms:', 'tel:', 'geo:'

Pre_condition:

  1. Make sure testapp_scheme.apk is installed;
  2. Make sure at least one Map Application is installed on device such as google map;
  3. Make sure network can be used.

Test steps:

  1. Click testapp_scheme app icon to launch the test app
  2. Click the link of "geo" to launch map app
  3. Click the link of "mailto" to launch email app
  4. Click the link of "open market" to launch Market app
  5. Click the link of "send sms" to launch message app
  6. Click the link of "make call" to launch Tel app

Expected Output:

  1. The app launched successfully
  2. The map app could pop up after click the link
  3. The email app could pop up after click the link
  4. The Market app could pop up after click the link
  5. The Sms app could pop up after click the link
  6. The Tel app could pop up after click the link
