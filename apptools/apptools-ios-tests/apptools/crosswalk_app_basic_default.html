Test precondition:

  1. Make sure the UUID of your iOS device is registered by developer account.

Test steps:

  1. Build a sample app with default mode(debug):
    $ crosswalk-app create com.example.hello
    $ cd hello & crosswalk-app build
  2. Connect your iOS device to your iMac, Open iTunes->"Your Device"->Apps
  3. Double click /path/to/com.example.hello/pkg/hello.ipa
  4. Click "Install" button of hello app on iTunes
  5. Click "Apply" button at the lower right corner of iTunes
  6. Check icon of hello app in your iOS device
  7. Launch hello app in your iOS device
  8. Check the homepage of hello app
  9. Close hello app via clicking the home button
  10. Long press the icon of hello app to uninstall it

Expected Results:

  1. Build the hello app successfully
  2. You can see app list of your iOS devices on iTunes
  3. The hello app is shown on the app list
  4. The button name changes from "Install" to "Will Install"
  5. App is installed on the device
  6. The icon of hello app shows well on the iOS device
  7. The hello app is launched
  8. You can see "This is a Crosswalk Sample Application" on the page
  9. The hello app is closed
  10. The hello app is uninstalled
